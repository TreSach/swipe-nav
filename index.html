Swipe Page Navigation

Introduction

Swipe Page Navigation is a Jquery functionality that refresh a different page. Like many smartphones, you can swipe left for next page or swipe right for previous page.


Getting Started

visit one of the How-To tutorials. On Desktop/PC, point your mouse to either left or right side of the webpage. Hold your left-side mouse then drag to the middle of the webpage. Let go the mouse and the page refreshes to the next page. Do the same way but in the opposite direction to return to the previous page. If you are using a mobile device, then you can just swipe your screen to refresh a different page.
